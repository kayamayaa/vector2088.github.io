Vector Cascade

The 1996 retrovirus event that permanently mutated human DNA across the globe.

CORE

Expressions

Superpowers; treated by society and government as heavily taxed, regulated biological functions.

CORE

Bloom Test

A mandatory, highly traumatic power-activation evaluation forced upon every citizen at age 12.

O.E.R.

Official Expression Registry

The ruthless, supranational global bureaucracy that regulates and taxes all mutated powers.

O.E.R.

O.E.R. Scale

The global power ranking system determining human value: Class E (Mundane), D (Utility), C (Hazardous), B (Strategic), and A (Omega).

V.A.S.T.

Vanguard Anti-Subversion Taskforce. Conscripted, heavily drugged elite anti-terror operatives celebrated as heroes.

V.A.S.T.

Phalanx

The standard 4-man V.A.S.T. squad consisting of a Striker, Anchor, Hound, and Tether.

BIO

M.E.I. (The Fade)

An incurable autoimmune disease that slowly destroys a person's powers, stripping them of societal value.

STATUS

Nulls (Blanks)

Powerless citizens legally stripped of their human rights and exiled from main cities.

Trackers

Mandatory subdermal O.E.R. GPS chips implanted during the Bloom Test.

TECH

Chrome / Amp-Rigs

Illegal cybernetics and lethal spinal pumps used to artificially boost mutant powers.
